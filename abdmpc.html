Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Law College

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Law College

Affiliation: Andhra University

Location: Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

Established: 1973

Overview:

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Law College is one of the premier law institutions in Andhra Pradesh, offering quality legal education with a focus on social justice and constitutional values. The college has produced numerous distinguished legal professionals and judges.

⚖ Law Programs Offered:

Undergraduate Programs:

  • 📜 5-Year Integrated BA LLB
  • ⚖ 5-Year Integrated BBA LLB
  • 🏛 3-Year LLB (After Graduation)

Postgraduate Programs:

  • 🧑‍⚖ LLM (Constitutional Law)
  • 💼 LLM (Corporate Law)
  • 🛡 LLM (Criminal Law)
  • 🌐 LLM (International Law)

Key Features:

  • Moot court hall for practical training
  • Extensive law library with legal databases
  • Regular seminars with eminent jurists
  • Legal aid clinic for community service
  • Excellent placement record in law firms and judiciary
  • Active participation in national moot court competitions

📌 Admission Information:

  • For 5-Year Programs: 10+2 with minimum 45% marks
  • For 3-Year LLB: Any Bachelor's degree with 45% marks
  • For LLM: LLB degree with minimum 50% marks
  • Entrance Exam: AP LAWCET for UG, AP PGLCET for PG
  • Application Period: March-May annually

🏛 Infrastructure:

  • Air-conditioned lecture halls
  • Digital library with legal research tools
  • Moot court room with video recording facility
  • Computer lab with legal software
  • Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls
