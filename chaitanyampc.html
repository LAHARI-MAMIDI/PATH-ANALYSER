Chaitanya Degree College

Affiliation: Andhra University

Location: Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

Website: chaitanyacollege.edu.in

Overview:

Chaitanya Degree College offers quality undergraduate programs with excellent academic standards and infrastructure. The college focuses on holistic development through its various degree programs and extracurricular activities.

🌟 Degree Programs Offered:

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc):

  • 🔢 Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (MPC)
  • 💻 Computer Science
  • 📡 Electronics
  • 📊 Statistics

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com):

  • 💰 B.Com (General)
  • 💹 B.Com (Computers)
  • 📈 B.Com (Taxation)

Bachelor of Arts (B.A):

  • 📜 History, Economics, Political Science (HEP)
  • 📚 English Literature
  • 🏛 Public Administration

Professional Degrees:

  • 📊 Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • 💻 Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Facilities:

  • Modern computer labs with latest software
  • Well-equipped library with digital resources
  • Career guidance and placement cell
  • Sports complex and indoor games facilities
  • Seminar halls for workshops and guest lectures

📌 Admission Information:

  • Eligibility: 10+2 pass in relevant stream
  • Application Period: May-June annually
  • Selection: Merit-based for most programs
  • Entrance: AUCET for some science programs
