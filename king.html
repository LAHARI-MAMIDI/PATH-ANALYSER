Overview:

King George Hospital (KGH) is one of the oldest and most reputed government hospitals in Andhra Pradesh. It is affiliated with Andhra Medical College and serves as a major center for clinical training. The hospital provides a strong platform for paramedical students to gain real-time clinical experience across various departments.
