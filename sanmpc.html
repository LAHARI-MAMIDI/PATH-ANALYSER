Overview:

Sanketika College of Art & Design is a premier institution for creative education in Andhra Pradesh. Established in 2005, the college offers specialized programs in visual arts, design, and creative technologies. With state-of-the-art facilities and industry-experienced faculty, Sanketika nurtures creative talent and prepares students for careers in the growing creative industries.

🎨 Design Programs Offered:

  • 🎨 B.Des - Bachelor of Design (4 years)
  • 🖌 BFA - Bachelor of Fine Arts (4 years)
  • 💻 M.Des - Master of Design (2 years)
  • 📐 Diploma in Applied Arts (2 years)

Specializations:

Design Streams:

  • Communication Design
  • Industrial Design
  • Fashion Design
  • Interior Design

Fine Arts Streams:

  • Painting
  • Sculpture
  • Printmaking
  • Photography

Unique Facilities:

  • Fully-equipped design studios and workshops
  • Digital fabrication lab with 3D printers
  • Photography studio with professional equipment
  • Material library and resource center
  • Exhibition galleries for student work

🎓 Admission Requirements:

For B.Des/BFA: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks + Design Aptitude Test
For M.Des: Bachelor's degree in any design field + Portfolio Review
