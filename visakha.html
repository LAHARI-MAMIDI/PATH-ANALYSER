Top Colleges for Paramedical / Allied Health

3. Visakha College of Nursing

NAAC Grade: B+

Location: Sheela Nagar, Gajuwaka, Visakhapatnam - 530012, Andhra Pradesh, India

Website: visakhanursing.org

Overview:

Visakha College of Nursing is a well-established institution in Visakhapatnam, committed to producing skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals. The college emphasizes both theoretical and practical learning through hospital-based training, advanced labs, and experienced faculty. It’s especially known for its nursing programs and allied paramedical education in supportive healthcare roles.

🌟 Specializations Available:

  • 🩺 B.Sc Nursing
  • 👩‍⚕ General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)
  • 🧬 Medical Lab Technician (MLT)

Facilities:

  • Well-equipped nursing labs with simulation mannequins
  • Clinical training in associated multispecialty hospitals
  • Hostel facilities and skill-based workshops
